Crime Report clause

Make sure you adhere to the following rules when reporting a crime:

  • Information provided (i.e. any crime you are reporting) MUST accurate to ease investigation.
  • If you have a record of false reports, your account will be terminated and you will be arrested.
  • When reporting a crime, make sure you include suspect(s) details such as Name, alias/nickname, where the crime occurred, date of crime. If you don't have any clue about such details, outline suspect description such as height, age group (e.g. youth, child, elderly).

NB: Crime reports will be sent to your local authorities, you may be called as a witness during trial.
