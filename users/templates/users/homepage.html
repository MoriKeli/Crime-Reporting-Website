Our Services

Security

Your security matters. As law enforcers, it is our duty to ensure you live, interact and work in a secure and harmonious environment.

Investigation

Crimes reported will be filed and local authorities will carry out investigation to arrest mentioned suspects.

Apprehension & Sentence

Arrested suspect(s) will be arraigned and charged ina court of law. Depending ont he judge's final word. The suspect may serve a jail term of the sated period of time and/or pay fine.
